About me

I'm a data analyst who genuinely enjoys digging into messy datasets and figuring out what's actually going on. I work with SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI - not because they're on a list, but because they help me build things that are useful: clean dashboards, automated reports, and analysis that people actually act on.

I care a lot about data quality. A wrong number in a report is worse than no number, and I've learned that the hard way across 4+ years working in enterprise systems at Capgemini, client projects in Dubai, and now an intensive analytics bootcamp in Berlin.

I work well with people across teams - whether that's translating a business question into a SQL query or walking a non-technical stakeholder through a Power BI dashboard. I'm based in Berlin, fluent in English, and conversational in German (B2).
